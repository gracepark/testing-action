# Viewing people's roles in an organization

You can view a list of the people in your organization and filter by their role. For more information on organization roles, see "[Roles in an organization](/organizations/managing-peoples-access-to-your-organization-with-roles/roles-in-an-organization)."

**Who can use this feature**: Organization members can see people's roles in the organization.
